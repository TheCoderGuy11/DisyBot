ABOUT US

Due to increase in the population of patient in the world, most of them died due to lack of awareness, money and time. So we will create a platform known as “DisyBot” which is based on the technology Artificial Intelligence, it will help the patient in finding the disease he is suffering from, through taking symptoms as an input, then it will calculate the possibility of the diseases and show the result. Add-on to this it will also show the treatment, cure and list of doctors who are the best in that field.
“DisyBot” is an Artificial Intelligence platform which is used in the field of medical science. DisyBot will help patient in finding diseases they have by simply entering the symptoms, cure and list of best doctors that deals with that disease. It is an Artificial Intelligence platform which will be divided in two modules that’s Front-End module which comprises with HTML,CSS and BOOTSTRAP and Back-End module comprises with Sqlite and Python.
DisyBot is based on many Technologies like – Data Science , Machine Learning , Artificial Intelligence and cloud computing. We will target the community of people suffering from any diseases. It will help people in finding the diseases without going to a hospital, so that he/she can start his treatment.
